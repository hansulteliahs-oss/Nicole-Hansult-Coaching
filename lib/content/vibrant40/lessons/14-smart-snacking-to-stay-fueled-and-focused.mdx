Snacking isn't the problem — mindless snacking is. When you're intentional about what you keep on hand, snacks can actually support your energy, blood sugar balance, and overall wellness without derailing your progress.

The key? Stock up on real food that works with your body, not against it. A smart snack includes protein, fiber, and/or healthy fats and actually satisfies you between meals.

Some of my favorite go-to smart snacks:

- Greek yogurt with berries — rich in protein and naturally sweet
- Veggies and hummus — crunchy, satisfying, and full of fiber
- A handful of almonds or mixed nuts — portable and nutrient-dense
- Lightly salted edamame — high in plant-based protein and fiber
- A protein bar — better than a Snickers, but check the sugar content
- Hard-boiled eggs — simple, satisfying, and easy to prep in batches

**Pro tip:** Don't let hunger make your decisions for you. Plan ahead. Keep smart snacks in your fridge, bag, or car so you're ready for the mid-day dip, travel delays, or post-workout recovery.
