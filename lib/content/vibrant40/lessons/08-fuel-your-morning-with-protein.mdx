Starting your day with 20–30g of protein can help you feel more satisfied, reduce cravings, support lean muscle development, and maintain steady energy levels — especially after the age of 40.

In this video, I share why protein in the morning matters and how to make it easy (yes, even on busy days!).

- Simple tips to boost protein at breakfast
- How much is enough
- Why it helps with energy, cravings, and confidence

Bonus: Want help choosing a protein powder? Read **Protein Powder 101**.

If you don't already, try adding 20–30 grams of protein to your morning this week and notice how you feel!
