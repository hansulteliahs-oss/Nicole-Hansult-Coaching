Need some easy ways to hit your protein goals in the morning?

Smoothies are one of the simplest options, especially when you're short on time but still want something nourishing.

Below are a few of my favorite high-protein smoothie recipes. They're quick to make, easy to customize, and packed with real ingredients to help you start your day feeling fueled and focused. Pick one and give it a try tomorrow!

#### Blueberry Banana Power Smoothie

Great for muscle recovery & brain health.

- 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
- 1/2 frozen banana
- 1 tbsp hemp or chia seeds
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
- Dash of cinnamon (optional)

#### Peach Turmeric Smoothie

Anti-inflammatory + protein boost.

- 1 cup frozen peaches
- 2 tbsp nut butter (almond or cashew)
- 1 tsp turmeric
- Handful of kale or spinach
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1 scoop clean protein powder

#### Green Detox Smoothie

Hydrating + packed with nutrients.

- 1/2 frozen banana
- 1/2 green apple
- 1/2 avocado
- 1 tbsp flaxseeds
- Handful of spinach or kale
- 1 cup coconut water or almond milk
- 1 scoop clean protein powder
