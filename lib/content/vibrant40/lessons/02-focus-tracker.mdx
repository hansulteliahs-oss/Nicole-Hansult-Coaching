Making change — especially in a short, focused program like this one — requires a little structure and a lot of consistency. That's where the Vibrant40 Focus Tracker comes in.

This simple, printable worksheet helps you:

- Clarify your daily priorities around movement, nutrition, and mindset
- Reflect on what's working (and what's not)
- Build accountability by checking in with yourself each day

You can fill it out in the morning to set your intentions or in the evening to reflect — it's up to you. Use it as a quick touchpoint to keep your goals front and center.

Download your Focus Tracker below and keep it somewhere visible. Let's make this momentum stick.
