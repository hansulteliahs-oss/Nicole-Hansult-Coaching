The biggest progress doesn't come from doing everything perfectly. It comes from doing something consistently.

Today, your win might be 5 minutes of movement, drinking one extra glass of water, or simply writing in your Focus Tracker.

That's enough.

Your goal isn't perfection. It's showing up for yourself, one small step at a time.

Write down today's win in your Focus Tracker and celebrate it. Small wins build strong habits.
