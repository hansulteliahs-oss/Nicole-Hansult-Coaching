Sleep changes after 40 — and so does what works to fix it. The 8-hours-or-bust advice ignores the hormonal shifts that actually drive 3 a.m. wake-ups and feeling unrested no matter how long you were in bed.

This lesson walks through what's happening biologically and gives you a short list of habits that genuinely move the needle: light, temperature, caffeine timing, and the wind-down anchor most women skip.

Recovery is where the work gets locked in. Treat it like training.
