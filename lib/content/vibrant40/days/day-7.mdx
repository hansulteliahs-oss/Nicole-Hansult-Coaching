Your body is changing. That's not the problem. The problem is the war most of us have been waging with it for thirty years.

This lesson is about the shift from war to partnership — not as a self-love platitude but as the only stance that actually makes the rest of this program work long-term. Hating your body into health doesn't last.

We'll talk about what acceptance looks like in practice, and why it doesn't mean giving up on feeling strong, capable, or vibrant.
