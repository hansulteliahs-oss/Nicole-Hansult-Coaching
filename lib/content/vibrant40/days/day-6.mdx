Perimenopause and menopause are not diseases to be managed away — but they're also not something you have to white-knuckle through.

This lesson explains what's actually happening hormonally, what symptoms are common (and which ones deserve a deeper look), and the conversations worth having with your doctor — including the ones where you may need to push back.

You'll leave knowing what to track, what to ask for, and what's worth trying before reaching for the next supplement.
