Telling a busy woman to "just relax" is useless. Cortisol doesn't respond to good intentions — it responds to specific inputs.

This lesson covers what cortisol actually does (and why chronic elevation drives belly fat, sleep disruption, and the wired-but-tired feeling), plus three nervous-system tools you can use in 60 seconds when you feel the spike.

Regulation is a skill. Today is where you start practicing it.
