Most diets fail women over 40 because they're built on restriction. This lesson gives you a simple framework: protein first, plants always, and timing that supports — not fights — your metabolism.

You'll learn what a vibrant plate actually looks like, why blood sugar matters even more after 40, and the three tweaks that move the needle without making food the enemy.

No meal plans, no measuring, no banned foods. Just a way of eating that you can keep doing.
