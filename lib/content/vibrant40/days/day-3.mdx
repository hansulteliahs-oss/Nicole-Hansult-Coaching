Movement after 40 is not about punishing yourself in the gym. It's about protecting bone density, building lean mass, and keeping your metabolism awake — with the smallest dose that actually works.

This lesson covers the two non-negotiables: strength training (two sessions a week, full-body, simple) and zone-2 cardio (the easy-conversation pace that builds your aerobic base).

You'll leave with a weekly skeleton you can actually keep — no two-hour gym sessions required.
