A Real-World Way to Eat Well: The Plate Method

## Body
You don't need to count calories. What's most important is to eat in a way that supports your health, energy, and body goals. One of the simplest ways to bring balance to your meals, without stress or tracking, is the Plate Method.

This visual tool helps you build a balanced plate that fuels your body, keeps you full, and supports your energy throughout the day.

Here's how it works:

**½ your plate: Non-starchy veggies**
Think leafy greens, broccoli, peppers, zucchini, mushrooms; anything colorful and packed with fiber, water, and nutrients. These help you feel full and support digestion.

**¼ your plate: Protein**
This is key for maintaining muscle, managing cravings, and supporting metabolism. Choose options like chicken, fish, tofu, lentils, pork or occasionally red meat.

**¼ your plate: Smart carbs**
Carbs aren't bad! Your body and brain need them! Go for options like sweet potatoes, quinoa, brown rice, or beans that give you energy and fiber.

Add a little healthy fat, like olive oil, avocado, or a handful of nuts, and you've got a complete, satisfying meal.

No apps. No numbers. Just a plate that makes sense and works in real life.
