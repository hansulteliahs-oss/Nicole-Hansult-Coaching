## Body
Movement as Celebration

For today's movement, repeat your favorite routine from the week — the one that made your body feel most alive, open, or grounded.

It might be the gentle stretches from Day 7, your strength sequence from Day 5, or even the mindful mobility from Day 2.

This isn't about pushing harder. It's about noticing how much more connected and capable you feel now than when you started.

"Notice how your body feels compared to Day 1.
 That's progress — and it's proof that consistency works."

Take these moments as a gift to yourself — gratitude in motion.
