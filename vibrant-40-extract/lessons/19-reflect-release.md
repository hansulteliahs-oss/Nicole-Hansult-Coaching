## Body
**Step 1: Reflect**

After watching the above video, open your Focus Tracker — the same one you started with on Day 1 — and turn to Section 3: Weekly Check-In.

Use these questions to guide your reflection:

Where were you most consistent this week?

What surprised you about your energy or mindset?

What did you notice about how you move, eat, or think about yourself?

What do you want to carry forward into next week?

Take your time here. This isn't about judgment — it's about awareness.
Awareness builds resilience, and resilience keeps your momentum going.

"The biggest progress doesn't come from doing everything perfectly. It comes from doing something consistently." — Nicole Hansult

**Step 2: Release**

Before you move on, take one deep breath.
Feel your feet grounded on the floor, your shoulders soft, and your breath steady.

You've made progress — and it's okay to let that sink in.
Give yourself permission to feel proud of the effort you've put in this week.
You're doing something that many people only think about doing.
